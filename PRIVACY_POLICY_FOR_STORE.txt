PRIVACY POLICY - Unified Game Launcher

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

SUMMARY
Unified Game Launcher is privacy-focused by design. All data stays on your computer. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

INFORMATION STORAGE
All information is stored LOCALLY on your device only:
- Game library data (paths, titles detected from your system)
- User preferences (theme, language, settings)
- Optional Steam ID (if you choose to sign in with Steam)

WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
We do NOT collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact details
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Any data about you or your computer

HOW INFORMATION IS USED
Locally stored information is used solely to:
- Display your game library within the app
- Remember your preferences
- Launch games when you request
- Organize your game collections

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Steam Integration (Optional):
- Uses Steam OpenID authentication (secure)
- Only Steam ID stored locally on your device
- No passwords stored or transmitted
- You can disconnect anytime

Free Games API:
- Fetches public game listings from GamerPower
- No personal information sent

Game Metadata:
- Fetches game artwork and information from SteamGridDB and RAWG.io
- Only game titles sent, no personal information

DATA SECURITY
- All data stored locally in: %APPDATA%\unified-launcher\
- No cloud storage or external servers
- Full user control over all data
- Uninstalling removes all data

YOUR RIGHTS
- Access your data (plain text JSON files)
- Delete your data (uninstall or delete data folder)
- Export your data (built-in export feature)
- Control optional features

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

CHANGES TO POLICY
Updates posted within app and store listing. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

INTERNATIONAL USERS
All operations local to your device. No data crosses borders.
