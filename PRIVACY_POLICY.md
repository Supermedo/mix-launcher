# Privacy Policy for Unified Game Launcher

**Last Updated**: January 18, 2026

## Introduction

Unified Game Launcher ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Unified Game Launcher stores the following information **locally on your computer only**:

- **Game Library Data**: Paths to installed games, game titles, and platform information detected from your system
- **User Preferences**: Your selected theme, language, display settings, and custom game collections
- **Optional Steam Account**: If you choose to sign in with Steam, we store your Steam ID locally

**Important**: All data stays on your computer. We do not transmit, collect, or store any personal information on external servers.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact details
- Payment information
- Location data
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Browsing history
- Any data about you or your computer

## How We Use Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:
- Display your game library within the app
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Launch your games when you request
- Organize your game collections

## Third-Party Services

### Steam Integration (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Steam:
- We use Steam's OpenID authentication (secure, industry-standard)
- Only your Steam ID is stored locally on your device
- We fetch your Steam library from Steam's API
- No passwords are ever stored or transmitted
- You can disconnect at any time

### Free Games API

The app fetches free game listings from GamerPower API:
- This is a public API that doesn't require authentication
- No personal information is sent to GamerPower
- Only game listings are retrieved

### Game Metadata Services

The app may fetch game information (covers, descriptions) from:
- SteamGridDB (for game artwork)
- RAWG.io (for game information)
- These services only receive game titles, not personal information

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: All your data is stored in JSON files on your computer
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not upload or sync your data to any cloud service
- **Full Control**: You have complete control over your data
- **Data Location**: `%APPDATA%\unified-launcher\` on Windows

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access Your Data**: All data is stored in plain text JSON files you can read
- **Delete Your Data**: Uninstalling the app or deleting the data folder removes all stored information
- **Export Your Data**: Use the built-in export feature to save your library as a backup
- **Control Data Collection**: You can choose not to use optional features like Steam integration

## Children's Privacy

Unified Game Launcher does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to organize game libraries and is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our store listing. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## Data Retention
