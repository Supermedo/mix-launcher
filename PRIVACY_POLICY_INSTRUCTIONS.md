## 📤 Where to Upload in Partner Center

### Method 1: Privacy Policy URL (Recommended)

If you have a website:
1. Upload `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` to your website
2. Get the URL (e.g., `https://yoursite.com/privacy`)
3. In Partner Center:
   - Go to "Properties"
   - Find "Privacy policy"
   - Enter the URL

### Method 2: Direct Text (Easier if No Website)

If you don't have a website:
1. Open `PRIVACY_POLICY_FOR_STORE.txt`
2. Update the contact information
3. Copy the entire text
4. In Partner Center:
   - Go to "Properties"
   - Find "Privacy policy"
   - Select "Paste text directly"
   - Paste the content

## 🌐 Quick Option: Use GitHub Pages (Free)

Don't have a website? Use GitHub Pages for free:

1. Create a GitHub repository (public)
2. Upload `PRIVACY_POLICY.md` as `README.md`
3. Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings
4. Get URL: `https://yourusername.github.io/unified-launcher/`
5. Use this URL in Partner Center

## 📝 Partner Center Steps

1. Open your app in Partner Center
2. Go to **"Properties"** section
3. Scroll to **"Privacy policy"**
4. Choose one:
   - **Option A**: Enter privacy policy URL
   - **Option B**: Paste privacy policy text directly
5. Click **"Save"**
